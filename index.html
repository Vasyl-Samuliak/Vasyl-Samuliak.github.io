THE LONG AND THE SHORT OF IT

I.A Solutions provides access, inspection and maintenance solutions to Construction, Industrial, Resource and Energy sectors throughout the Asia Pacific. A New Zealand owned and operated company delivering world class results with friendly and down to earth service.

I.A Solutions offers industry leading safety standards by using IRATA qualified technicians, and strictly operating to the ICOP guidelines. With highly trained and certified technicians we can provide an integrated asset maintenance program to suit our client’s needs safely, efficiently and on time.

We pride ourselves on providing exceptional service with honesty, integrity and our clients best interests at the forefront of all our operations. Industry experience, cutting edge technology and innovative access techniques set us apart and ensure our clients are provided with intelligent, flexible and economical access, inspection and maintenance solutions.
